TECHNIQUES

  • Variable Selection: Using cutting edge machine learning tools to identify key variables (out of thousands of possible candidates) that might affect educational performance negatively.
    • these tools are often used for stock market predictions and the fields of genomics and econometrics
  • Spatial Analysis: We analyzed how the relationships between educational performance and the key variables vary across geographies.
  • Spatial Profiling: We described the infrastructure constitution of areas that are susceptible to lower educational performance.
  • Spatial Autocorrelation: We quantified the relationship between key variables and infrastructure systems.
